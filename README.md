## 🧠 Research

### 📑 A Comprehensive Analysis of HateMM Dataset: Exploring Deep Learning and Comparative Approaches for Multimodal Hate Speech Detection

Presented at **E2ACON 2025**, hosted by **NIT Jalandhar** and co-hosted by the **School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Newcastle University, Singapore**.

This research explores multimodal hate speech detection techniques using deep learning approaches to improve accuracy and scalability in AI-driven content moderation systems. The work highlights the importance of multimodal fusion for responsible AI applications.
